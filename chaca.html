    Turmeric Fried Fish with Dill Leaves

    Cá chiên nghệ thì là

    Anecdote

    lorem ipsum

    Ingredients for 4 people

  • 500g of mackerel fillet

    250ml of oil

    4 whole red onions

    2 whole eggs

    1 slice of ginger

    1 tbsp of cornstarch

    1/2 tbsp of brown sugar

    1/2 tbsp of fish sauce

    1/2 tsp of turmeric powder

    1/2 tsp of sesame oil

    A bunch of dill

    A bit of pepper

    A bit of salt

    • Preparation Instructions

    1. Cut the mackerel into cubes of 3cm long.

      Put the chopped mackerel pieces and all the ingredients, except the slice of ginger in the blender, and blend them well.

      Once that mix is well-blended, put it in a bowl.

      Wrap the bowl with a plastic wrap and let the mix marinate in the refrigerator for 2 hours.

      Cut the ginger into small strips, 5 minutes before you take out the blended mixed from the refrigerator.

      Take out the bowl, unwrap the plastic film and fry the marinated mackerel until it turns into a brownish-yellow color. Make sure to have enough space in the pan or fryer, if you are going to fry every single pieces. If not, do fry 4-5 pieces at a time. Use your judgement to determine the amount needed to be cooked throughly.

      Once the mackerel pieces are nicely cooked, serve them on a bed of dill and sprinkle some ginger strips on it.
